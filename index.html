About Us

Cedar Academic Services is an academic and research services organization. While CedarAcad started formally in 2023, work started in 2020 with freelance scientific writing, editing and bioinformatics analysis. The focus of this organization are disciplines in Social, Life, and Medical Sciences. With working partners and mentors across many disciplines, we offer various services including research assistance, scientific writing and editing, statistical and bioinformatics analysis.

Our Services

Bio-informatics:

We carry out whole genome sequence and metagenomics analysis for students and researchers interested in microbiome studies and microbial ecology

Scientific writing and editing services:

We assist with writing, editing, and proofreading academic papers, theses, dissertations, and articles.

Research assistance:

We support with literature reviews, data collection, and research design.

Statistical analysis:

We assist with statistical data analysis, interpretation, and visualization.

Formatting and publication support:

We help with formatting academic papers, for publication.

Online course support:

We assist with online course design, development, and delivery

Academic integrity support:

We offer resources and guidance on avoiding plagiarism, proper citation, and academic honesty.
