About Us

Cedar Academic Services is an academic and research services organization registered with the government of Nigeria with SMEDAN registration number; SUIN385659372697. While CedarAcad started formally in 2023, work started in 2020 with freelance scientific writing, editing and data analysis. The focus of this organization are disciplines in Life Sciences, Health, and Medical Sciences. With working partners and mentors across many disciplines, we offer various services including research assistance, scientific writing and editing, biostatistical data analysis.
